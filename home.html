BRHS and PantherVenue

The Bridgewater-Raritan High School, established in 1957, plays a vital role in Somerset County of New Jersey. The PantherVenue, our premier gymnasium, seats up to 3,500 and boasts modern amenities, including a high-quality sound system, adjustable lighting, and a large video screen. It was renovated in 2024, and features updated bleachers, a resurfaced floor, and new scoreboards, making it the ideal choice for sports, graduations, and community events.

Policies

Important rules and guidelines for using PantherVenue.

  • General
  • Tickets

Please review these policies to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience at PantherVenue.

No Outside Food

Outside food and beverages are prohibited to maintain cleanliness and support venue concessions.

Bag Checks

All bags are subject to search upon entry for safety and security purposes to ensure a safe environment.

No Smoking

Smoking, including e-cigarettes, is strictly prohibited inside the venue to maintain air quality.

Quiet Zones

Designated quiet zones are available for guests who wish to relax or have a private conversation.

Alcohol

Alcoholic beverages may not be consumed at PantherVenue. Doing so will result in immediate removal.

Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed on the premises, with the exception of service animals.

Parking

Parking is available in designated lots. Parking fees apply depending on the event.

Lost & Found

Items lost during an event can be found at the lost and found desk located near the main entrance.

Non-Refundable

Tickets are non-refundable unless the event is canceled by the venue, in which case full refunds will be issued.

Purchase Limit

A maximum of 10 tickets can be purchased per transaction to ensure fair distribution.

Lost Tickets

Lost or stolen tickets can be replaced at the box office with valid ID, but be sure to report it immediately.

Transferable

Tickets are transferable, but you must notify the box office of any changes in ownership prior to the event.

Special Discounts

Special discounts may be available for students and military personnel. Valid ID is required for verification.

VIP Tickets

VIP tickets offer additional perks such as early entry, exclusive seating, and access to VIP-only areas.

Group Tickets

Group ticket packages are available for parties of 10 or more at discounted rates. Please contact support.

Cancellations

If an event is canceled, ticket holders will receive refunds or the option to exchange tickets for future events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn everything you need to know about PantherVenue.

  • Where is PantherVenue located?

    Finding us is simple. PantherVenue is located at 600 Garretson Road, inside Bridgewater-Raritan High School in Bridgewater, New Jersey. For turn-by-turn directions, please visit the "Contact Us" section at the bottom of our home page, where you will find an integrated Google Maps tool.

  • What is the process for purchasing event tickets?

    To purchase tickets, you must first create an account and log in. Once logged in, you can navigate to the "Events" page, select your desired event, and click "Register." This will open a customized box office where you can complete your secure purchase. A confirmation and your tickets will then be available in your user dashboard.

  • How do I rent the venue for a private event?

    We would be delighted to host your event. The "Rent Venue" page provides all the necessary information and directions for submitting a request. You can fill out our multi-step form to provide details about your event, and our administrative team will review your submission. You can track the status of your request directly from your user dashboard.

  • Is this website optimized for mobile devices?

    Yes, PantherVenue is designed with a mobile-first approach to ensure a seamless experience on any device. The layout, navigation, and all interactive features automatically adapt to fit your screen, whether you are using a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

  • How is my personal information kept secure?

    We take your security very seriously. All user data, including login credentials and rental requests, is managed through Google Firebase, a secure and reliable backend service. This ensures that your personal information is handled according to the highest industry standards.

  • What is the downloadable event flyer?

    On the day of an event for which you have purchased a ticket, you will be able to download a personalized event flyer from your dashboard. This flyer includes a unique QR code for fast and easy check-in, as well as essential event details and directions, providing maximum convenience for our guests.

  • What can I do from my user dashboard?

    Your personal dashboard is your central hub for managing your PantherVenue experience. From here, you can view your ticket purchase history, see your upcoming events, check the status of your venue rental requests, and access your event-day flyers.

  • What is the Admin Dashboard?

    The Admin Dashboard is a private, secure portal for PantherVenue staff. It serves as our command center for operations, allowing administrators to review and approve rental requests, manage the public event calendar, and monitor key site analytics to ensure we are always improving our services.

  • How does the 360-degree virtual tour work?

    Our 360-degree tour provides an immersive, interactive view of the gymnasium. You can access it from the button on our home page. This feature allows potential renters and guests to explore the space from their own device, giving them a true sense of the venue's scale and features before they even arrive. It's the next best thing to getting in-person directions and walking through the doors yourself.

  • Can I get directions from the AI Chatbot?

    Yes, our AI assistant, PantherBot, is fully trained on all venue information. You can ask it for the address, parking information, or any other questions you may have. PantherBot is available 24/7 by clicking the chat icon at the bottom-right of your screen.
